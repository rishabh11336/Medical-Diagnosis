About Me

Hello, I'm Rishabh Singh, a passionate Python developer and creator of the Python web app using Flask. I specialize in building innovative and user-friendly applications that leverage the power of artificial intelligence.

One of my notable achievements is integrating the ChatGPT API into my Flask app to serve as a reliable and intelligent helpdesk. By utilizing the capabilities of natural language processing, I have developed a chatbot that can assist users in addressing their health-related concerns.

With the help of a user-friendly interface, users can select their specific health condition and indicate the severity of their condition through dropdown menus. The chatbot then communicates with the ChatGPT API, which provides intelligent responses tailored to the given condition and severity. This ensures that users receive accurate and relevant information, addressing their concerns effectively.

By combining the power of Flask, Python, and the openAPI, I have created an interactive and personalized experience for users seeking health-related guidance. I believe that technology has the potential to revolutionize the healthcare industry, and my goal is to contribute to this transformation by developing innovative solutions.

If you're looking for a talented Python developer with expertise in Flask, AI integration, and web application development, I would be delighted to collaborate with you. Feel free to reach out to me for any inquiries or project opportunities.
